## Who I work with

I work with high-performance and developing athletes, coaches, and organizations who want decision-quality information about training and performance.

## What I do

- Applied sport science and testing
- Strength and conditioning programming and support
- Performance data analysis and dashboarding
- Needs analyses and KPI frameworks

## Background

- Sport scientist and S&C coach working with bobsleigh, skeleton, hockey, and other sports.
- Experience building testing batteries, KPI models, and dashboards for national-level programs.
- Graduate training in kinesiology and sport medicine with a focus on coordination, performance analysis, and applied statistics.

## How I work

- Clarify the performance question.
- Build or refine the testing and monitoring approach.
- Create simple, decision-ready outputs for coaches and athletes.
- Iterate based on feedback and new data.
