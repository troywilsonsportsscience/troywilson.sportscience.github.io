## Problem

The program needed a consistent way to test and monitor athletes across camps and seasons.

## Method

- Needs analysis with staff
- Selection of key tests and metrics
- Centralized data structure and dashboard

## Outputs

- Standardized testing battery
- Simple reporting for staff and athletes

## Outcome

Improved alignment between training decisions and objective data.
