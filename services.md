## Overview

I offer applied sport science, strength and conditioning, and data analysis services that can be combined into packages or delivered as standalone support.

### Major service areas

#### 1. Testing & Monitoring

- Jump testing (force plates, contact mats, or field-based)
- Isometric strength assessments
- Speed and sprint profiling
- Monitoring frameworks and KPI selection

#### 2. Strength & Conditioning

- Program design aligned with sport demands
- In-person or remote coaching
- Off-season, in-season, and return-to-play considerations

#### 3. Data Analytics & Dashboards

- Building performance databases and pipelines
- Dashboards for coaches and organizations
- Custom scoring systems, rankings, and reports

#### 4. Consulting & Education

- One-off or ongoing consulting for teams and organizations
- Workshops and education sessions for staff and athletes
